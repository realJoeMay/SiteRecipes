Chicken and Sausage

and rice

Ingredients

2 lb Chicken Thighs
1 lb Ground Sweet Italian Sausage
1 Sweet Onion
1 Red Bell Pepper
2 cups Jasmine Rice
10 oz Grape Tomatoes
1 bunch Green Onions
32 oz Chicken Stock

Staples

2 tsp Olive Oil
3 tbsp Minced Garlic
¼ cup White Wine
2 tsp Paprika
2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Black Pepper

Instructions

1
Cut the chicken into small pieces. Season with salt, pepper, garlic, and paprika. Cook in a large pan with olive oil. After cooking for a few minutes, add the Italian sausage, breaking it up in the pan.
2
Chop onion and pepper, and add them to the pan, along with the minced garlic. Once the onions are translucent, add the white wine and let it boil.
3
Add the rice directly to the pan. Let it cook for a minute before adding the chicken stock. Let it come to a boil.
4
Turn heat down to medium low and cover for 15 minutes. Scrape the bottom of the pan every few minutes to keep the rice from burning.
5
Chop the green onions and cherry tomatoes. Add them to the pan and cook for a few minutes before removing from heat.
