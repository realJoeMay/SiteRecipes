Creamy Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

3 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
1 Sweet Onion
4 tablespoons Minced Garlic
1¼ cup Chicken Broth
1¼ cup Heavy Cream
¾ cup Parmesan Cheese
1 lb Fettuccine

staples

4 tablespoons Flour
2 teaspoons Salt
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
½ teaspoon Black Pepper
5 tablespoons Olive Oil
2 tablespoons Butter

Steps

1
Season the chicken with salt, garlic powder and pepper. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour, 1/4 cup parmesan cheese. Dredge chicken in the flour mixture.
2
Heat 2 tablespoons of oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Swirl pan to coat evenly.
3
Fry chicken breasts until golden on each side, cooked through and no longer pink (about 4-5 minutes each side). Transfer to a plate and set aside.
4
Reduce heat to medium. Sauté the onion in the remaining oil/juices in the pan until softened.
5
Add the remaining oil to the pan and heat through, mixing it through the onions. Sauté minced garlic about 2-3 minutes. Add the broth to deglaze the pan. Scrape up any browned bits and let simmer and reduce to half, about 5 minutes.
6
Reduce heat to medium-low. Pour in the cream. Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer for about 3 minutes
7
Mix in 1/2 cup parmesan cheese. Continue cooking gently for about 3 minutes until cheese melts, while stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to your taste.
8
Add the chicken back into the pan and let simmer for a further minutes to thicken the sauce to your liking.
9
Serve over pasta.
