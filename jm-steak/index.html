JM Steak

with baked potato and zucchini

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

2 lb
Steak
4
Golden Potatoes
2
Sweet Onions
1
Zucchini
1
Yellow Squash

Staples

1/4 cup
Parmesan Cheese
2 tbsp
Olive Oil
2 tbsp
Butter
1 tsp
Thyme
1 tbsp
Garlic Powder
3 tsp
Salt
3 tsp
Black Pepper

Instructions

1
Dry off steak with a paper towel. Brush with olive oil, and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Let it sit and approach room temperature while you move on.
2
Place the trivet and 1 cup cold water to the instant pot. Prep the potatoes by pricking with a knife and place them on the trivet. Set to high pressure and cook for 18 minutes. When done, allow pressure to release naturally for 8-10 minutes, then turn the valve to release any remaining pressure.
3
Chop onions and cook with 1 tbsp olive oil in a cast iron pan. When nicely caramelized, transfer to a nonstick pan. Add 1 tbsp butter and vegetables. Cook for 10 minutes, then remove from heat.
4
Turn the heat to medium high. Put the meat in the skillet. If the steak has a fat cap, sear that side first. Let each side sear for 2 minutes. then use the tongs to sear the edges all the way around. Add a dollop of butter on top and let cook for 2-3 minutes, depending on thickness.
5
With the pan still hot, add the vegetables back into the cast iron pan and top with parmesan cheese.
