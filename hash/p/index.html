Breakfast Hash

Ingredients

6 Eggs,
½ yellow onion,
1 red bell pepper,
20 oz shredded hash browns,
8 oz Breakfast Sausage,
¾ cup cheddar cheese,

Staples

3½ tbsp butter, divided
1 tsp Salt,
1 tsp Black Pepper,

Instructions

1
Crumble and cook the sausage in a large skillet.
2
Put the cooked sausage in a bowl and set aside. Melt 3 Tbsp butter in a over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper and onion and saute 3 minutes.
3
Toss potatoes in the mixture and spread into an even layer, season with salt and pepper to taste. Let cook tossing only occasionally until starting to brown about 6 to 7 minutes.
4
Toss in cooked sausage and cook until potatoes are tender, about 3 minutes longer.
5
Push mixture to one side of the pan. Reduce heat to low. Melt remaining 1/2 Tbsp butter in empty half of skillet and pour in eggs. Season with salt and pepper, scramble and cook.
6
Toss mixtures together, sprinkle with cheese and toss a few times.
