Guacamole

with stuff in it

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

3
Haas Avacadoshalved, seeded and peeled
2
Tomatoesseeded and diced
1/2
Oniondiced
1 tablespoon
Cilantrochopped
1
Limejuiced

Staples

1 clove
Minced Garlic
1/2 teaspoon
Cayenne
1/2 teaspoon
Ground Cumin
1/2 teaspoon
Salt

Instructions

1
In a large bowl place the scooped avocado pulp and lime juice, toss to coat.
2
Using a potato masher add the salt, cumin, and cayenne and mash. Then, fold in the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and garlic.
3
Let sit at room temperature for 1 hour and then serve.
Source: Alton Brown
