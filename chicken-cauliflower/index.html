Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 425. Start the rice on the stove, follow directions.
2
Break apart the cauliflower and add to a large bowl. Add olive oil, salt, pepper, and thyme. Toss to coat and then spread into a single layer on the baking sheet and start baking on the higher shelf. Cook 10 minutes.
3
Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Place the chicken on a baking sheet, and put in the oven.
4
Cook the chicken for 10 minutes. Then move to the top rack and broil for 5 minutes.
