French Toast

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

4
Eggs
¼ cup
Milk
½ tsp
Vanilla Extract
1 tsp
Cinnamon
8 slices
Brioche Bread
½ cup
Maple Syrup

Instructions

1
In a 10-inch or 12-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk together cinnamon, eggs, milk, and vanilla and pour into a shallow container.
2
Dip bread in egg mixture. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side.
3
Serve with syrup.
Source: Robert Irvine
